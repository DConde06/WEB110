The 2026 World Cup will be hosted by the United States, Mexico, and Canada. 16 total cities will be hosting matches. It will be the first edition of the tournament with 48 teams. The world cup starts in June and will end in July. The final is set to played on the 19th oif July. Check out the Fifa website to recieve information on how to apply for tickets.
