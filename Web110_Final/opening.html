Hosts Mexico will open the biggest-ever edition of the World Cup on 11 June 2026. The opening game of the 2026 FIFA World Cup will be played in Mexico City at the iconic Estadio Azteca, featuring co-hosts Mexico vs. South Africa, a repeat of the 2010 opener, kicking off the first-ever 48-team tournament across North America.
