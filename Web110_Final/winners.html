Only eight countries have won a world cup


1. Brazil

Brazil has won 5 titles (1958, 1962, 1970, 1994, 2002)

2. Germany

Germany has won 5 titles (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014)

3. Italy

Italy has won 4 titles (1934, 1938, 1982, 2006)

4. Argentina

Argentina has won 3 titles (1978, 1986, 2022)

5. France

France has won 2 titles (1998, 2018)

6. Uruguay

Uruguay has won 2 titles (1930, 1950)

7. England

England has won 1 title (1966)

8. Spain

Spain has won 1 title (2010)
